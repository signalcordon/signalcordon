Frequently asked questions
What if the brief doesn't lead to a contract?+
The brief delivers verified intelligence and ready-to-send outreach — the result depends on your follow-through and technology fit. We do not guarantee contract awards. If you add the success fee agreement, the $500 advance is non-refundable, but no further fee is owed unless a contract results from a path we identified.
What industries do you cover?+
Any company with dual-use technology relevant to DoD priorities. We cover autonomous systems, biotech, advanced manufacturing, audio and communications, defense software, materials science, directed energy, and supply chain logistics. If your technology solves a real problem, we can find the DoD path to it.
Can I request focus on a specific program office or vehicle?+
Yes. Use the optional notes field in the request form to specify priorities — SOCOM, AFWERX, DIU, a specific SBIR track, or a named program office. We factor your guidance into the brief without limiting the search to only what you specify.
How is this different from searching SAM.gov myself?+
SAM.gov shows you what is posted. SignalCordon finds the connection between what you do and what is posted — then verifies it, ranks it by fit and timeline, and writes the outreach. The gap between "there is a solicitation" and "here is the path, the person to contact, and the exact words to use" is what SignalCordon closes.
What happens after I receive the brief?+
You receive three ready-to-send outreach emails — one per contract path — with the warm hook named in sentence one and your specific value in sentence two. Review, personalize if needed, and send. If you want ongoing monitoring and weekly outreach drafting, the retainer tier covers that.
